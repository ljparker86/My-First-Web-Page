The Parker Homestead

The House

The house was built in the early 1900's. It needs a lot of work, but it has a solid foundation

The Barn

The barn was full of old cow crap and straw. It took a while, and with some help from a friend and a tractor, we were able to clean out the main part of it.

Animals


We have started off with egg laying chickens, but hopefully next year, we will have a couple of pigs. I wanted to get goats, but we are not set up for them just yet.

The Field


We have a field lot on the east side of the barn. Last spring I tried to make it into a sunflower field. However, the birds ate most of the seeds, and the weed took over most of it. Now my plan is to start a garden, and maybe it will grow to most of the field. I also plan on putting the pigs out there.
